## 1) Story height-related GFA calculation rules
### CN
- Label: **full**; Threshold: >= 2.20 m
- Label: **half**; Threshold: 1.20 m to 2.20 m
- Label: **excluded**; Threshold: < 1.20 m
### HK
- Label: **excluded**; Threshold: < -
- Label: **half**; Threshold: -
- Label: **full**; Threshold: >= -
### US
- Label: **full**; Threshold: >= 3.0 feet
- Label: **half**; Threshold: -
- Label: **excluded**; Threshold: < 3.0 feet
### EU
- Label: **full**; Threshold: >= 2.50 m
- Label: **half**; Threshold: 1.50 m to 2.50 m
- Label: **excluded**; Threshold: < 1.50 m
